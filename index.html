CHAR SIU-OVEN STYLE BAKED RIBS

                         These Char siu-style oven baked ribs are the most delicious at home creation you can serve to your family anytime of the year!

Ingredients:

  • Ribs (Pork or Beef)
  • Green onion
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Five spice powder
  • white pepper
  • Sesame oil
  • Shaoxing wine
  • Soy Sauce
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Molasses
  • Red food coloring

Steps:

  1. Rinse the ribs and thorougly pat them dry with paper towels
  2. Mix all ingredients well ( sugar,salt,five spice powder,white pepper,sesame oil,Shaoxing whine,soy sauce,hoisin sauce,molasses and red food coloring to make marinade.
  3. Reserve about 1/3 of the marinade and rub the ribs with the rest of it.
  4. Allow ribs to marinate for at least 2 hours for the best results or overnight.
  5. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees and put the ribs on a rack resthing on a baking sheet.
  6. Roast for 30 minutes then Remove from the oven and baste with marinade and repeat the process 2 more timees.
  7. You'll see the sugars in the marinade create that signature crust and perfect flavor.
  8. Let the ribs rest for 5 minutes to allow juice to redistribute Sprinkle Green onion and Enjoy!
